About R1 Premium

Establishing a legacy of luxury, safety, and durability in residential and commercial buildings across Hyderabad.

Who We Are

Securing Homes, Optimizing Utilities

R1 Premium was founded with a singular focus: to modernize structural home safety and space-saving systems. High-rise apartment balcony structures present safety challenges for families with young children or pets. Traditional iron grill designs block ventilation, airflow, and crucial outside views.

By engineering high-tensile stainless steel invisible safety grills, we solved this dilemma. Today, R1 Premium is a trusted name across Hyderabad, offering not just protection, but also premium ceiling-mounted stainless steel cloth hanging systems that maximize balcony laundry spacing.

We operate out of Saroornagar and service communities wide—from high-rises in Gachibowli and HITEC City to residential villas in Jubilee Hills and Banjara Hills.

100% In-House

No subcontractors. All works are handled by trained R1 Premium technicians.

Rigid Testing

Every grill cable is tensioned and weight-tested before handover.

Direct Factory Pricing

By sourcing materials directly, we avoid distributor markups, passing savings to you.

Why Customers Trust Us

Professional Installation

We never cut corners. Our technicians are certified to handle heavy-duty anchor drilling in concrete walls. Cables are tensioned using calibrated industrial tools to ensure safety parameters are exactly met.

Premium Materials

We strictly utilize authentic marine-grade SS316 stainless steel for invisible safety cables and rust-resistant SS304 for clothes drying pipes. Our fittings are corrosion-proof, ensuring a long structural lifecycle.

Customer Satisfaction

Our relationship does not end after drilling. We provide detailed walkthroughs on how to operate pulley rods and smart motorized hangers, and back our installations with active maintenance support.
