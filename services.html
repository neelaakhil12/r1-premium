Balcony & Window Protection

Premium Invisible Safety Grills

R1 Premium invisible grills provide an absolute security boundary for modern homes without turning your beautiful balcony into a cage. We construct them utilizing marine-grade SS316 stainless steel wires, which are highly tensioned and encased in a custom nylon coating for ultimate rust and weather resistance.

They are highly recommended for children safety and pet fall protection in multi-story apartments and villas across Hyderabad, offering an elegant structural appearance.

Child & Pet Safety

Prevents accidental falls with tensioned structural cables.

Smart Drying Solutions

Premium Stainless Steel Cloth Hangers

Maximize your balcony utility area with R1 Premium's ceiling and wall-mounted space-saving cloth hangers. These configurations allow you to dry clothes in an organized manner by pulling the hanger up to ceiling height, utilizing high-level warm air currents for speedier drying and keeping your floor space clear.

Built using heavy-duty SS304/SS316 stainless steel rods that support thick laundry loads without bending, operated with smooth pulley string mechanics.

Space Saving Design

Lifts laundry to the ceiling, releasing critical floor space.

Faster Air Drying

Utilizes high-level heat currents for quick, natural drying.

Available Models & Configurations

Manual Pulley Hanger

Individually operated stainless steel rods controlled with nylon cords. Raise and lower each pipe as needed.

Setup: 1-2 Hours

Fixed Ceiling Hanger

Fixed frame directly attached to ceiling concrete. Strong and sturdy construction meant for high load items.

Setup: 1 Hour

Wall Foldable Hanger

Accordions out from the wall. Folds flat when not in use. Ideal for balconies without ceiling access.

Setup: 30-60 Mins
